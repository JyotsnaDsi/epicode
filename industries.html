EPICODE CIP

Epicode CIP is a multi-channel Communications and Data management Platform that will Help you to achieve a clear competitive advantage by allowing you to focus on your business goals rather than having to deal with the difficult task of managing a multi-vendor customer engagement solution.

EPICODE IVRS

The IVRS technology has been around for a long time and has evolved continuously over the years. Epicode IVRS uses a well tested, widely deployed and stable IVRS platform that allows customers to build customized self help call flows for customers to access routing information and services without having to speak to a customer support agent. This also helps enterprises to achieve significant cost savings and competitive advantage.

Epicode IVRS can be seamlessly integrated with third party CRM, Databases, Payment gateways and core banking applications to increase the overall utility of the IVRS and cater to a large customer base resulting in cost savings to the enterprise.

CLOUD TELEPHONY ENEBLERS

Contact centers use both inbound and outbound calling abilities for effective management of callers. Outbound campaigns can make use of a powerful media processing capability that enables the detection of answering machines / fax tones and live human voices on outbound calls and communicates the detected information to the external application.

iraCPA offers CPA based on advanced voice activity detection algorithms and offers significant benefit to contact centers.
