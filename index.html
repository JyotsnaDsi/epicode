Featured Solution

IraHive

IraHive is an interprocess messaging framework with redundancy and load balancing features.

Architects and Programmers always wish for a seamless way to communicate between programs, without resorting to complicated APIs.

The wish list also contains automatic service discovery, message priority, RSA encryption, high availability, selective buffering if the destination is not ready, or sent by simple name or role, and sent to one destination or many or all. IraHive was designed to cater to all those needs using very simple and lightweight APIs.
