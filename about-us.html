ABOUT EPICODE

Epicode’s core team brings together 70+ years of combined work experience in developing Advanced Communication Platforms

We are a like minded team of professionals, mostly engineers, passionate about developing niche and robust software based communication products and solutions that can contribute to the continuous evolution of technology and add to the competitive advantage of our enterprise customers and partners.

As part of our company culture we take our social responsibilities seriously and make sure that we adhere to progressive policies of gender equality, ethical business practices and comply with the local laws and regulations of different countries where we operate.

VALUE

The new age enterprises are using digital technology broadly for two purposes; Firstly, to create a service delivery platform and secondly for customer engagement, a combination of third party applications like CRM, Contact centre and Business Intelligence software.
Epicode’s middleware products can help enterprises fill the gaps in their communication infrastructure, optimize their service delivery applications and support their transition from an on-premise to a hybrid & pure cloud digital infrastructure.

MISSION

To develop products that can address key technological gaps in the fast evolving digital applications space that is transitioning from an on-premise to a hybrid and pure cloud deployment model.

VISION

To create a world class technology products and solutions company with a collaborative work culture and a customer centric approach.
