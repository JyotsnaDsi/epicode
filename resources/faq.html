General Questions

Does Epicode CIP support a blended agent licensing scheme ?
Yes, we offer a blended agent license where the agent can be part of both inbound and outbound processes and can be assigned to either or both the queues in real time.
Do you support cloud based or on-premise installation?
Our products support both on-premise and cloud based installation with a flexible licensing scheme.
What are the encryption mechanisms supported by your products?
All the products can be configured to be accessed via only Secure Socket Layer (SSL) which uses RSA encryption and Public Key Infrastructure (PKI). This provides protection from packet sniffing and eves-dropping. Voice products support both encrypted signaling known as SIPS which can be SSL or TLS with signed certificates, as well as encrypted audio/media known as SRTP.
Do you support integration with CRMs through CTI Connectors?
Integration with various CRMs such as Salesforce, Leadsquared, Microsoft Dynamic 365, vTiger, Zoho are provided with ability to push data to CRMs on various call events and also show the relevant customer info to agents as part of screenpop.
Does Epicode CIP support a hybrid cloud architecture?
Epicode Hybrid Cloud CIP allows enterprises to take advantage of pushing more applications on the cloud while complying with the TRAI regulations. A combination of VOIP servers deployed on-premise and web applications and CRMs deployed in the cloud helps in setting up the hybrid cloud architecture.
How do you provide integration with Outbound Campaign managers?
We provide support to add telephony integration to campaign managers with the help of APIs. A rich set of APIs using HTTP POST help to achieve the complete telephony integration.
How do we use your AMD, CPA module in our applications?
You can integrate your application with our IraCPA module using our APIs and websockets integration methods to enable AMD.
Do your products support deployment on AWS, Azure or similar platforms?
Yes, all our products can be deployed on cloud platforms and achieve auto scaling or geographic redundancy.
Are your products compliant with Data Security Standards?
Yes, all our products are compliant with general data security requirements. Our Contact Center Solution is certified to be PCI-DSS compliant.
Do you support a SaaS model?
We can support the SaaS business model depending on the business case. Please contact us to discuss.
Does Epicode CIP have an in-built Lead Management System?
No. However, Epicode has full fledged integrations with leading Lead Management Systems like Lead Squared and Zoho CRM. Integration with other LMS systems can also be considered depending on the business case.
What are the OS versions that are supported?
All our softwares can be installed on servers running Linux(Debian 10) or Windows (Windows Server 2016 or newer). Our agent desktop application is available for Windows(7, 8.1, 10) or Debian (8, 9, 10)
Have you deployed Epicode CIP for the Banking and Financial sector (BFSI)?
Yes, Epicode CIP has been deployed and used by leading BFSI customers across the globe.
What is the max call handling capacity in a single node?
A single entry level enterprise server can handle a capacity of 1000 simultaneous calls with 100% recording.
