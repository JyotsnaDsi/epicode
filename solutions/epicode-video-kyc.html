Video Know Your Customer (KYC)

Video enabled real time customer verification solution from Epicode

"Remote verification of customers in real-time"

Video KYC is a new concept that is being adopted to enable government agencies, Mobile service providers, Financial services companies and other businesses who need to implement the “ Know Your Customer” norms as part of their customer enrolment process. Epicode Video KYC solution adds the video functionality to the Unified Agent UI to enable agents to verify the customer credentials through visual authentication and also to acquire customer documents in real time
