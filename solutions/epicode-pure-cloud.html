EPICODE Pure Cloud CIP

"Epicode CCIP enables enterprises to seamlessly shift their Contact center infrastructure to data centers on the cloud"

With the advent of large Cloud data center providers like Amazon AWS, Microsoft Azure and Oracle Cloud service providers, more and more enterprises and even application providers are transitioning their on premise IT infrastructure to Cloud. The final push came from an unexpected and sudden explosion of a global Pandemic starting this year!

Epicode’s Cloud Customer Interaction Platform is a proven cloud ready platform that has been successfully deployed on AWS, Azure and Oracle Cloud. The trunk manager module of Epicode’s CCIP seamlessly integrates with any SIP trunk provider like Amazon Chimes, Twilio, Vonage or any other country specific SIP trunk provider.

Global enterprises and organizations with operations across the world can now deploy Epicode’s CCIP on centralized AWS or Azure cloud data centres to manage their customer care process and route their Inbound customer calls to country specific toll free numbers acquired from local SIP trunk service providers. Enterprises can also route the customer calls over the internet using VOIP to offshore customer support centers through a secure VPN connection.

Epicode’s CCIP is a multi channel contact center Platform that can handle both inbound and outbound process and supports ready CTI connectors to leading Cloud CRMs like Salesforce, Zoho, Freshdesk and others providing an enterprise a one-stop solution to handle all their customer care requirements.
